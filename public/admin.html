How the worklist works

Every 🔲 marker imported from the corpus becomes a row here — a specific cultural question that hasn't been validated yet.

When your Haiti partner returns with an answer, find the gap, click ✓ Resolved, and note how it was validated. The knowledge then goes into the corpus as a confirmed entry.

Gaps that turn out not to matter can be dismissed. This keeps the list focused on what actually needs field work.
